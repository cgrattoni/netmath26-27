Before You Begin

Keep the actual NetMath application open next to this tutorial. The fields below are locked examples and reminders only. You cannot type into this tutorial.

This page is deliberately read-only. The form fields in this walkthrough are locked or disabled so you do not accidentally think you are submitting anything here.

What to Have Ready

  • Your name exactly as it appears in Infinite Campus.
  • Your school email address and a reliable parent or guardian email address.
  • Your current unweighted GPA on a 4.0 scale from Infinite Campus.
  • Your course choice: Math 241 for Multivariable Calculus, or Math 257 for Linear Algebra.
  • The official NetMath form open at apps.citl.illinois.edu/NetMathRegistration/NetMathForm.

Plan ahead for tuition. The course costs District 211 students $300. After October 31, 2026, this tuition payment becomes non-refundable.

Scholarship option: The Jerry Uhl Scholarship can help cover tuition. The 2026-2027 application deadline is July 24, 2026 at 11:59 p.m. CST. If you plan to apply, ask your Calc BC teacher in person for a recommendation letter before summer break, then follow up by email.

After You Submit

This final step is required for the Fremd side of your registration.

Submitting the UIUC NetMath form does not complete your Fremd registration. After you submit, watch your school email for the PDF copy of your application.

Jerry Uhl Scholarship

The Jerry Uhl Scholarship is available to help cover NetMath tuition for Partner High School students. Fremd students have a strong history of earning this scholarship.

  • The scholarship deadline is July 24, 2026 at 11:59 p.m. CST.
  • The application requires a teacher recommendation letter.
  • If you plan to apply, ask your Calc BC teacher in person before you leave for summer.
  • After asking in person, follow up with an email so your teacher has the details in writing.
  • Apply if the scholarship would help your family plan for the $300 tuition.
